Magi Software

Magi Software is a professionsal I.T. consultancy with over 20 years experience in delivering software solutions in a wide range of domains and industries including ecommerce applications, the banking sector and military solutions.


Services

Agile Software Development

Magi Software has years of experience working on Agile Software Projects. Whether you need someone to work on an Agile Software development team or someone to lead the team in a Scrum Master role, Magi Software can help.

Consultancy

Magi Software provides a consultancy service to come into your business and provide support and guidance for the whole of the software lifecycle. This could be anything from couching to recommending best practices.

Databases

Magi software has years of experience working on small and large scale database applications. We can offer help with traditional databases such as Oracle and MySql as well as NoSql databases such as Mongo and Couchbase.

Bespoke Software

Magi Software is able to create bespoke software from start to finish. Whether its a small desktop application or large web based soltions, we can help.

Training

Magi Software can provide all your training needs from in house training and support to formal education. We have experience of providing training in industry as well as supplying courses to educational institutions.
